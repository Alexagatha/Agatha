Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What types of cakes do you offer?

We offer a variety of cakes including wedding cakes, birthday cakes, cheesecakes, chocolate cakes, red velvet cakes, carrot cakes, fruitcakes, cupcakes, and tiramisu.

Do you offer custom cake designs?

Yes, we specialize in custom cake designs for weddings, birthdays, baby showers, and other special occasions. Contact us for more details.

What are your business hours?

We are open Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Our physical store is closed on weekends, but our online store operates 24/7.

How can I place an order?

You can place an order through our online store or visit our physical store during business hours. For custom orders, we recommend contacting us in advance.

Do you have an option for payment on delivery?

We do not have that option as a 50% deposit is required before production and the balance is to be made before the cake is delivered.

Do you offer training?

Yes, we conduct both online and physical classes. Contact us for more information on our training programs.
